In the upcoming Fall of 2024, I will be starting my PhD at the [University of Massachusetts Amherst](https://www.umass.edu), in the [Department of Mathematics and Statistics](https://www.umass.edu/mathematics-statistics).

I am working in the area of [higher category theory](https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/higher+category+theory) and [categorical homotopy theory](https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/Categorical+Homotopy+Theory). My main focus is [homotopy type theory](https://homotopytypetheory.org/book/) and [(higher) categories](https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/higher+category+theory), but I like to think about topics and questions that heavily uses [category theory](https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/category+theory) in general.
