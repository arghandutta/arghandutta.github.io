Welcome

I am a graduate research scholar in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Ottawa, Canada. From Fall 2024 to Fall 2025, I was a graduate teaching assistant in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, USA. I completed my MS in mathematical sciences from the Department of Mathematics at the Indian Institute of Science, India, in July 2024.

I am working in the area of higher category theory and categorical homotopy theory with Martina Rovelli.
